Our Services

Application Development

Cal-SE is providing a full range of application development services for new product development and re-engineering existing products to new platforms and for maintenance services by conducting Rapid Product development life cycle management services aiming to accelerate the creation of software products, reduce time to market, and help predict schedules.

Cloud Computing Services

At Cal-SE, our cloud computing experience and skilled vendor-agnostic resources will help you choose the most appropriate cloud computing services for your business environment. Vendors Collaboration: Amazon Web Service, Windows Azure, Google App Engine.

Mobility Development

Cal-SE provides professional mobile application development and porting services for any requirement of clients. It has designed to fit client’s requirements and objectives that enable to have instant experience of mobility technology with availability, access and real time features on smart devices on almost platforms including iOS, Android, and Windows Phone OS.

Software Quality Assurance

Cal-SE has been involved in software testing services since we started our business in 2007. We provide our clients strong and dedicated QA teams for full range of professional testing services, taking complete testing responsibility by establishing testing methodologies, creating test cases or plans, and performing automation testing.
